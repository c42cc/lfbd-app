How It Works

This is your private voice companion. You talk, it listens and responds. Your conversations are saved between sessions so it remembers what you've shared.

Starting a Conversation

Tap the microphone button at the bottom of the screen. Your browser will ask for mic permission the first time — say yes. Once connected, just start talking. You'll see a status indicator ("Listening...", "AI speaking...") so you always know what's happening.

When you're done, tap the mic button again. Your conversation saves automatically.

The Buttons

Mic
Tap to start talking. Tap again to stop and save.
History
See past conversations. Tap one to re-read it. Check the box to give the AI that context next time.
Type
Opens a text box. If you'd rather type something mid-conversation instead of saying it, use this.

Choosing a Voice

At the bottom of the screen, you'll see a toggle to switch between two AI voices:

Gemini Grok

Both work the same way — pick whichever one you prefer. If you switch while a conversation is active, it'll end the current session first.

Giving It Context

The AI doesn't automatically remember past sessions. But you can give it that memory.

  1. Tap the History button to open your past conversations
  2. Check the box next to any conversations you want the AI to remember
  3. Start a new conversation — the AI will have that context and can reference what was discussed

There's a limit to how much context fits, so if you select many long conversations, earlier parts may be trimmed.

Settings

Tap the gear icon in the top-right corner. You'll see two fields:

System Prompt — This shapes how the AI behaves. The default is a compassionate listener who keeps responses short and warm. You can change it to anything you want.

Message Prompt — Extra info that gets included in every session. Things like your name, what you're working through, or anything you don't want to have to repeat each time.

Hit Save when you're done. Changes apply to the next conversation you start.

Your Privacy

Your voice is never recorded or stored. Only the text of what was said is saved.

Conversations are stored privately on the server and are only accessible through your personal link. No one else can see them.

You can delete any conversation from the History panel by tapping the × next to it.

Tips

Add it to your home screen. On iPhone, open this link in Safari, tap Share, then "Add to Home Screen." It'll open like a regular app — no browser bar, no tabs.

It's okay to sit in silence. The AI won't rush to fill pauses. Take your time.

You can stop anytime. Tap the mic to end. Everything saves.
